♠ Privacy Policy

PRIVACY POLICY
Last updated: July 13, 2026

The short version: Screen Stakes doesn't collect your data. Everything stays on your phone. We can't see it, we don't want it, we couldn't sell it if we tried — it never leaves your device.

WHAT THE APP STORES (ON YOUR PHONE ONLY)
• The name you enter at setup
• Your wallet balances, streak, and game results
• Your settings and daily-reset date

All of this is saved in the app's local storage on your device. It is never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with anyone — including us.

WHAT WE DON'T DO
• No accounts. You never give us an email, phone number, or password.
• No analytics or tracking of any kind.
• No ads and no ad networks.
• No selling, sharing, or transferring data — there's nothing to sell.
• No third-party services receive anything from this app.

SCREEN TIME (FUTURE FEATURE)
A future version will connect to Apple's Screen Time system to measure app usage and block apps when a wallet hits zero. Apple's rules require that this data is processed entirely on your device — it is technically prevented from leaving your phone, and we will never see which apps you use or for how long. This policy will be updated before that feature ships.

FRIENDS & MULTIPLAYER (FUTURE FEATURE)
A future version may use Apple's Game Center and, with your permission, your contacts to help you find friends. Contacts will only ever be read with your explicit permission, used only to show you who's playing, and never uploaded to any server we operate. This policy will be updated before that feature ships.

THE WAITLIST (WEBSITE ONLY)
If you join the waitlist on our website, we keep your email address in a private list used for exactly one thing: telling you when Screen Stakes is available. It is never sold, shared, or used for anything else, and it's deleted on request — reply "remove me" to any email from us. This is the only personal data we hold anywhere, and it comes from the website, not the app.

YOUNG PEOPLE
Screen Stakes is built for teenagers and collects no personal data from anyone, of any age.

DELETING YOUR DATA
Delete the app, or tap "reset everything" in the Menu. Because nothing ever leaves your phone, that's genuinely everything — there is no copy anywhere else.

CHANGES
If the app ever changes how it handles data, this policy will be updated first and the change will be called out in the app.
